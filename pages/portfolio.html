99minutos Re-design

Project developed during Coderhouse's advanced UX/UI design course. For this project, I redesigned the interface of a web page in charge of managing e-commerce shipments in different countries of Latin America. During the process, many UX methodologies were applied, such as benchmarking to know the strengths and weaknesses of the competition; heuristic evaluations to know the points of improvement of the page; interviews before and after starting the redesign to always know what users think; We also work with lean ux canvas, journey maps, tree testing, etc.
